What is Category Research ?

Category Management is a strategic approach to align organization’ resources to perform focused procurement whereby segmenting organization’ spend into areas which contain similar or related products or services together.

Category Management may involve bundling products into direct and indirect products or services or may buckets products or services further by volume, value, supplier or type. Later, using various associated theories, such as Pareto (80/20 rule), Kraljic matrix, and ABC analysis, fathom insights about these categories purchasing behaviours and help organizations priorities and focus their spending and efforts.

How we do it ?

We support our customer with building categories and developing Category analysis in this process identifying the cost drivers across the supply chain.

We understand ingredients supply chain and generate insights from their price trends/cycles. We provide value advise on ‘buy’ or ‘wait’ decisions and similarly on extent of forward covers decisions.

We manage our customer categories budgets and delivery cost saving for bottom-line.

We build scenarios and ‘What-if’ analysis using our Know-how of supply chain and craft our customers supply chain strategies.

What are the benefits of these initiatives?

Organizations can use category management as an systematic approach to reduce procurement cost at organization level.
